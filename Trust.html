Ink and Blood


Determined to clear my name, I chose to trust Detective Williams and work closely with her. As we delved deeper into the investigation, I realized that my insights into the mind of the fictional killer could indeed be of help. Together, we uncovered leads and connections that could lead us to the real-life murderer. Our collaboration proved fruitful as we discovered a pattern in the killings that matched the order of events in my novels. Each murder recreated a chapter from one of my books, and it became clear that the killer was systematically following my stories. We had to delve into the twisted psyche of a fan who had taken their admiration too far. With Detective Williams' experience and my knowledge of the killers I had created, we closed in on the obsessive fan who had brought my darkest imaginings to life. Working together, we had a better chance of stopping this reign of terror.
