Ink and Blood


Frustrated by the slow progress and growing distrust of Detective Williams, I decided to pursue a lead independently. I felt I could make more headway on my own without anyone doubting my innocence. My investigation led me to a local bookstore, where I found a copy of my latest book with cryptic annotations. The obsessive reader, it seemed, was meticulously studying my novels for clues and hints. This was a breakthrough, and I was determined to track down this fanatical admirer to understand their motivations better. Alone, I had the advantage of not having to explain my every move or be questioned by Detective Williams. I felt a growing sense of responsibility to put an end to this nightmare on my own terms, to prove that I was not responsible for these gruesome crimes.
